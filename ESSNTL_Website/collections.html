Our Pricing Strategy

We follow a **value-based pricing** model. Our prices are not set by competitors, but by the tangible value we provide. This includes our investment in material science, sustainable manufacturing, and timeless design. We believe in creating products that last, offering our customers a lower "cost-per-wear" than fast fashion. Our model ensures fair margins that can be reinvested into the R&D that makes our products unique.
